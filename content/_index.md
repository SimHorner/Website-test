Welcome! I am Simeon.

I have always wanted to be better at writing; being able to make people laugh, but also to convince, anger and intrigue. I never got into the swing of diarising but am hoping this gives me the platform to write more, on different topics. My aim is to keep a track of books read, projects started and ideas thought (worth writing down).

I grew up in London, studied Maths at Edinburgh and now work as an account manager for retailers in the IT industry. Outside of work, most of my time is spent training in either Jiu-Jitsu or Judo but also enjoy cooking, detective books, DIY, traveling and dancing.
