Book Review: The Martian

June 9, 2026

Andy Weir’s The Martian is a fast-paced survival story set on Mars, following astronaut Mark Watney as he tries to stay alive after being stranded.

What I Liked

  • The humour — Watney’s voice keeps the story light even when things go wrong.
  • The science — grounded enough to feel real without slowing the pace.
  • The tension — every small problem feels huge when you’re alone on a planet.

Final Thoughts

A clever, funny, surprisingly uplifting sci‑fi novel about problem‑solving under pressure.
