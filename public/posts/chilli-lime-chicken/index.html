Chilli Lime Chicken

A bright, zesty chicken dish that works perfectly on the grill or in a hot pan.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs, bone-in, skin-on
  • 2 limes (zest + juice)
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • Salt + pepper

Method

  1. Mix lime zest, lime juice, honey, soy sauce, chilli flakes, and garlic.
  2. Coat the chicken and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Grill on medium-high heat for 6–8 minutes per side.
  4. Rest for 5 minutes before serving.

Notes

Serve with rice, grilled corn, or a crunchy salad.
