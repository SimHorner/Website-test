Three Days in Rome

Rome is one of those cities that hits you immediately — the heat, the noise, the history layered on every street corner.

Day 1 — The Classics

I started at the Colosseum, walked through the Forum, and ended up on Palatine Hill.
The scale of everything is overwhelming, and the views at sunset are unreal.

Day 2 — Vatican + Trastevere

The Vatican Museums are intense but worth it.
Afterwards, I crossed the river into Trastevere for dinner — easily the best food of the trip.

Day 3 — Wandering

No plan. Just coffee, gelato, and getting lost in side streets.
Rome rewards wandering more than any other city I’ve visited.

Takeaway

Three days isn’t enough, but it’s a perfect introduction.
